Palak (Spinach) -Tofu

ingredients : Five serving

Spinach 3 can (8 Oz)
Tofu 1 extra firm [16 Oz)
Shahi paneer Masal (1 tbsp)
tomato puri [8 Oz can]
salt (1/2 tsp)
olive oil (1 tbsp)
Chilli powder (1/4 tsp)

Receipe:
Heat 1 tbsp oil in Fry pan [3 Quator]
add paneer masala and chilli-power wait for a minutes
add tomato can to it and heat for another 3 minutes 
add sinach can [5 min]
add tofu and cook for [10 minutes] or untill water dries
serve warm.
